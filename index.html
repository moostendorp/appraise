Our Appraisal Process

  • Initial Consultation

    We begin by discussing your needs, property details, and the purpose of the appraisal to provide the best service.

  • Proposal & Agreement

    After assessing your requirements, we provide a detailed proposal. Once you approve, we move forward with signing the agreement and securing a downpayment to initiate the process.

  • Property Inspection

    Our certified appraiser visits the property to take measurements and assess its condition.

  • Market Research & Valuation

    By gathering detailed property data, analyzing current market trends, and comparing similar properties, we apply the most suitable valuation method, whether it's the cost, market comparison, or income approach, ensuring accuracy and relevance.

  • Report Preparation & Delivery

    A detailed appraisal report is prepared and delivered to you.
